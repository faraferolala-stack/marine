CoralGuard Swarm

Intelligent mini robots working together to protect marine life, clean microplastics, and restore coral reefs through the power of swarm technology.

Explore Our Mission

Our Mission

We aim to protect the ocean by deploying a swarm of smart, fish-like robots that detect and collect microplastics without harming coral reefs. Inspired by nature, our robots communicate like schools of fish, sharing data and making real-time decisions to safeguard marine ecosystems.

Eco Sensors

Each robot monitors water quality and coral health using advanced sensors.

Smart Swarm

They communicate wirelessly, coordinating movements and sharing discoveries.

Solar Energy

Powered by solar panels for long-term, eco-friendly underwater missions.

Data for Science

Collected information helps scientists fight pollution and climate change.

Our Vision

The CoralGuard Swarm is more than a technology—it's a movement to heal our oceans. By combining innovation, sustainability, and teamwork, we can restore coral reefs, protect marine life, and inspire the world to care for our planet.
